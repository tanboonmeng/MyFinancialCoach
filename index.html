Why this exists

You know the theory. Now get a kickstart.

Most young Singaporeans have already heard the advice — save an emergency fund, get insured, start investing. But advice isn't a plan. The MAS Basic Financial Planning Guide sets the benchmarks; what's missing is someone to turn them into your next small step — and to check you actually took it.

The usual experience

  • Scattered articles and calculators
  • One-off advice with no follow-through
  • Jargon that assumes you already know

With your coach

  • One clear level at a time
  • Weekly nudges that build the habit
  • Plain English, grounded in MAS benchmarks

Coach chat

Ask your coach anything about the basics

Your coach is a chatbot grounded only in official sources — the MAS Basic Financial Planning Guide, MoneySense articles, and HDB home-financing pages. Ask in plain English; it answers from the guide and points you to the right official tool when a figure changes.

  • Grounded in 29 official MoneySense & MAS sources — and it always cites them
  • Explains your dashboard targets in plain English, never re-does the math
  • Won't recommend products or stocks — it points you to a licensed adviser
  • Only asks for the basics — never your NRIC, bank details, or passwords
