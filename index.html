Let's set up your financial coach.

It takes about a minute. You'll set up your own Telegram nudges so your coach can check in on you each week, then jump straight into your first level.

Set up your own nudges

Weekly check-ins come from a Telegram bot that you own and control. Creating one takes a few minutes:

  1. Create your bot. Message @BotFather on Telegram, send /newbot, follow the prompts, and copy your bot token.
  2. Add the token. Paste it into your n8n Telegram credential (or your Python scheduler config).
  3. Register yourself. Open a chat with your new bot and send /start.
  4. You're set. Your weekly nudges now come from your own bot.

Your bot, your token, your data — nothing runs through anyone else's account.

You're all set! Your coach is ready.
